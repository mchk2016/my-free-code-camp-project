CHAPTER 1: A, AN—not, without; unusual or irregular


Study the sample sentences below before doing the following exercises.

agnostic He had lost his former faith and had become an agnostic.

amoral Infants are amoral because they have not learned the difference between right and wrong.

anarchy The overthrow of the government resulted in anarchy.

anecdote The speaker began his talk with an entertaining anecdote.

anemia Her weakness was caused by anemia.

anesthetic Before the operation, he was given anesthetic.

anomaly As a bird, the ostrich is an anomaly in nature. It cannot fly.

anonymous The donor of the new hospital wing wished to remain anonymous.

asymmetrical At the fashion show, some designers featured asymmetrical hemlines.

atheist As an atheist, she objected to the nativity scene in the town square at Christmas.

atypical A classical concert performed by a rock band would certainly be atypical.
